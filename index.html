usage

https://www.youtube.com/feeds/videos.xml?channel_id=UC1Zfv1Zrp1q5lKgBomzOyCA
becomes
https://enteral.pi-wo.pl/feeds/videos.xml?channel_id=UC1Zfv1Zrp1q5lKgBomzOyCA
